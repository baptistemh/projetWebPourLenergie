Our Story

2005

Prototype and creation of our Eco2mix website with a team of three ECE engineers specialized in environment and energy.

Our Goal

In a context where energy prices are exploding and where the population wants to consume in a more environmentally friendly way. It seemed to us to be essential to create a site allowing the user to know the origin of his energy.

5.6 Million Euros

This is the amount of money we received from our investors at the creation of the site. Almost 20 years later, our capital is more than 56 million euros, which is almost a x10.

679 653 Clients

It is the number of customers who trust us today. So why not you?
